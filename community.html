Did you know?

Yam Zone, is a place in Rivers State located at Creek Road Market in the heart of Port Harcourt. 50,000 tubers of yam come into Yam zone everyday averaging nothing less than 250,000 tubers of yam in a week (on a 5 working days count).


Let us assume a tuber of yam is sold for at least #400.00 (Nigerian Naira), this implies that in a week we spend up to #100,000,000 (100 million) on yam alone. In a month the cost is over #400,000,000 and the annual amount spent on yam is over #4.8billion all in Yam zone, Port Harcourt. If only we could leaverage on this opportunity by focusing a little on our agriculture and food crop production, we could turn expences into income and improve our economy.
